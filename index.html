Kids Read with Libby!

Kids and their reading buddy can borrow ebooks, audiobooks, magazines, and more from your local library for free! Libby Kids is the newer library reading app by OverDrive, and linked to the Libby app which is loved by millions of readers worldwide!

It uses utility classes for typography and spacing to space content out within the larger container.
